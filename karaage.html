Chris' Karaage Recipe

This is my delicious, bite-sized, fried-chicken that I love to make for potlucks and gatherings. An incredibley flavorful dish that pairs well with most things and a fried food that happens to be free of gluten thanks to the potato starch!

This dish usually takes about 1-2hrs to make depending on the amount (always a good idea to make extra as this one disappears quickly). Expect about an hour of prep time and about 30min to an hour of cook time.

Ingredients:

For this recipe you will need the following:

  • Chicken thighs with bone-in and skin-on
  • 1 medium onion
  • Tangerine peel (any citrus will do!)
  • Garlic (fresh)
  • Ginger (fresh)
  • Sake or rice wine vinegar
  • Sesame oil
  • Black pepper
  • Salt
  • Garlic (powdered)
  • Potato starch
  • Rice bran oil
